Mini Masters GIVE YOUR LITTLE ONES A JUMP START WITH ART! Fridays 10:30am-11:15am Ages 2-5 years old $10/class or 5 class pass for $46 10 spots available each week so call or book today! Children who are immersed in art at an early age develop a stronger perceptual awareness of the world around them, laying the foundation for needed critical thinking skills. Each week’s project introduces a master artist and new technique using a wide variety of nontoxic art media, while inspiring their imagination and strengthening their observational skills through storytelling and music. Parent/guardians are encouraged to participate. Select from the menu below o choose one or all of the upcoming Mini Masters classes.
