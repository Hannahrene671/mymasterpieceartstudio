Adult Partys

Schedule a private event to celebrate a special occasion. Plan an awesome Team Building or Office Event that your co-workers will love.

The kids can’t have all the fun, so we have designed the Arty Party for adults too! Host a birthday, holiday or family celebration; bridal or baby shower; a party of friends; or a girls/guys night out! “Because life is too short for bare walls”.

We have designed perfect art experiences for adults to create their own masterpieces.We’re here to show you how to make art, make fun, and make memories. Let us help you select the perfect art project for your group and then just relax and enjoy the art experience as an instructor takes you through a fun art lesson for your event while you sip wine, chat with friends, and have a great time creating your masterpieces!

Instructed Party

Paint on canvas or sculpt with clay.

Let our professional instructors take you through a painting or clay lesson. Co-workers will enjoy relaxing, sipping wine, and having fun as we take them through a paint lesson from start to finish. choose from one of the images here or call us for a custom lesson. Minimum 10 -maximum 40 people
$20 per person
Includes all materials and firings for clay.

Paint on Canvas

Includes instruction and all materials. For these projects each participant will paint using acrylic on canvas. A wide array of color selections are available for each participants to make their art personal. Paintings may be taken home the day of your event!

Sculpt with Clay

Includes instruction clay, slip glaze and 2 firings. Clay projects take about 2 1/2 weeks to complete firings. For these projects each participant will hand-build a mug or platter, emboss, applique and glaze. Let each person make their own. Or for even more fun draw names and have another group member make one for their co-worker!

Simply select the art lesson from the menu (we can also customize a lesson just for you)

Exploration Party

This is a great option for larger groups or groups who would like to include families: Paint, sculpt and draw at your own pace, create anything you like or make a game of it with each person choosing an idea card to inspire their subject matter. Each participant receives a package containing a large 18×24 cold pressed paint paper, paper for chalk pastels, sulfite drawing paper for the light table and 1 pound of potters clay plus firing.

Minimum 10 – maximum 60 people $16 per person for up to 20 $14 per person over 20 Please call or email for your reservation Please plan to provide any food or drink your group would like. Wine glasses are available for your use.

A deposit of $75 is required for your party reservation A two week cancelation is required for deposit refund. A gratuity for your instructor or host is not included in the above pricing.
