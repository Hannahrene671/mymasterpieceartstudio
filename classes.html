Portfolio Classes

In our signature class series, your child will learn various art techniques and styles with step-by-step instruction. Using acrylic paint, collage, chalk, pastels, clay and glaze— this class series is a great introduction to art. A variety of fine art subject matters will be introduced and explored such as landscape, still life, figures, portraits, and animals.

Homeschool Portfolio Classes

Our most popular class series has expanded to better serve children in homeschool programs. In this Wednesday class your child will learn about new techniques in drawing, painting, and sculpting. Studio Drawing

Shape, movement, value, and color.

5-week series $98 3-week series $55 (December and May) These classes fill up quickly, so call or book online today! If Wednesdays don’t fit your schedule, you can always book a private lesson.

Studio Drawing 1

Learn traditional drawing techniques and theory using graphite, charcoal, pastels and inks. Students will study perspective, form, value, composition, and drawing from observation.

Studio Drawing 2

For the student who has had prior drawing classes and is ready to take their skills to the next level! This class continues with traditional drawing techniques while the subject matter is driven by the student teacher collaboration.
