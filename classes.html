Studio Drawing 1

Learn traditional drawing techniques and theory using graphite, charcoal, pastels and inks. Students will study perspective, form, value, composition, and drawing from observation.

Studio Drawing 2

For the student who has had prior drawing classes and is ready to take their skills to the next level! This class continues with traditional drawing techniques while the subject matter is driven by the student teacher collaboration.

Wheel Works

Centering, opening, pulling, and glazing.

5-week series $160 3-week series $96 (December and May) 4 spots available each series, so call or book today! If these times don’t work you can always schedule a private lesson.

Intermediate Wheel, Beginning Wheel, Homeschool Wheel. Wheel Works students learn the basics of clay and pottery wheel techniques including positioning for throwing on the wheel, centering, opening, pulling, shaping and trimming. In addition to these skills, students will acquire a Wheel Pass which allows them to use the pottery wheel in the Open Studio to work independently on their skills while following studio protocols.

Mini Masters

Give your little ones a jump start with art!

Fridays 10:30am-11:15am Ages 2-5 years old $10/class or 5 class pass for $46 10 spots available each week so call or book today! Children who are immersed in art at an early age develop a stronger perceptual awareness of the world around them, laying the foundation for needed critical thinking skills.

Each week’s project introduces a master artist and new technique using a wide variety of nontoxic art media, while inspiring their imagination and strengthening their observational skills through storytelling and music. Parent/guardians are encouraged to participate. Select from the menu below o choose one or all of the upcoming Mini Masters classes.
