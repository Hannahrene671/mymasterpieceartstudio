Art Hour

Have an hour of free time? Come make some art!

Every Saturday from 1:00-2:00pm Ages 6 to adult. Yes you can take this class with your kids! $16/class 12 spots available each week so call or book today!

The Art Hour is a fun way to learn new art skills and create beautiful projects. You will use a variety of media including acrylic paint, charcoal, pastels, chalk, clay and more! This instructed workshop is for ages 6 to adult, so bring the whole family!

For Adults

Masterpiece Wine and Paint Nights

$28/person See the Canvas, Clay and Carafe schedule for these fun workshops!

These evening events are perfect for gathering with friends, making a date night, or for trying your hand at art in fun environment!

What makes MMAS Wine and Paint Nights unique?

Our instructors have Bachelor’s Degrees in the Fine Arts and they will take you through an evening of fun and creativity. Through their expertise, they will help you customize your projects to your tastes and before you know it, you’ll have something you’ll be proud to display in your home.

We also understand that creativity matters and we will always guide you through an art lesson allowing you to select colors that reflects your personal taste. Quality instruction without the cookie cutter result!

We love art and want to share our knowledge with you in a fun and relaxed environment!

Choose from our current schedule of art nights shown below or create your own paint and wine night…It’s easy! Just select the project/date and reserve your spot. Each event is $28 per person and this includes instruction, all materials, and up to two glasses of wine or other available beverages. We have a minimum of 8 and can accommodate up to 25 for your private art night.

Team Building & Office Events

We’ll make your corporate/office gathering creative, fun and easy! Think of us for your next corporate event, office party, team-building workshop, reunion, or fundraiser!

Stage a fun, no stress office party!

Bring your group together and add a dose of creative fun to your next team-building or holiday event!

We are pro’s at party giving, and we have the perfect party for your corporate event! Sip wine, paint a masterpiece or sculpt with clay… celebrating with art is a great way to entertain your group, create camaraderie, nurture creativity and of course, each person will come away with a finished piece of art. No need to be shy, absolutely no experience is needed.

How it works . . it is easy!

Select and confirm a date and time (plan on 1 1/2- 3 hours for your event)

Select the style of Art Party that best fits your group:

Instructed Party

Paint on canvas or sculpt with clay.

Let our professional instructors take you through a painting or clay lesson. Co-workers will enjoy relaxing, sipping wine, and having fun as we take them through a paint lesson from start to finish. choose from one of the images here or call us for a custom lesson. Minimum 10 -maximum 40 people
$20 per person
Includes all materials and firings for clay.

Paint on Canvas

Includes instruction and all materials. For these projects each participant will paint using acrylic on canvas. A wide array of color selections are available for each participants to make their art personal. Paintings may be taken home the day of your event!

Sculpt with Clay

Includes instruction clay, slip glaze and 2 firings. Clay projects take about 2 1/2 weeks to complete firings. For these projects each participant will hand-build a mug or platter, emboss, applique and glaze. Let each person make their own. Or for even more fun draw names and have another group member make one for their co-worker!

Exploration Party

This is a great option for larger groups or groups who would like to include families: Paint, sculpt and draw at your own pace, create anything you like or make a game of it with each person choosing an idea card to inspire their subject matter. Each participant receives a package containing a large 18×24 cold pressed paint paper, paper for chalk pastels, sulfite drawing paper for the light table and 1 pound of potters clay plus firing.

Minimum 10 – maximum 60 people $16 per person for up to 20 $14 per person over 20 Please call or email for your reservation Please plan to provide any food or drink your group would like. Wine glasses are available for your use.

A deposit of $75 is required for your party reservation A two week cancelation is required for deposit refund. A gratuity for your instructor or host is not included in the above pricing.
